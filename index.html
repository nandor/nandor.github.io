Nandor Licker

I am a first year PhD student in the Computer Architecture group, under the supervision of Dr. Timothy M. Jones. I research static binary optimisations. I am a member of Trinity College.

Presently, I am working on enabling cross-language optimisations between OCaml and LLVM in order to inline external calls, exposing opportunities for further optimisations.
